About Tadpole Doctor


  • - World wide many groups of frogs are in danger of extinction

  • - A key threat to frogs is the spread of infectious disease

  • - One newly identified disease infects tadpoles

  • - This disease has been detected in the UK

  • - As a community, we can track this disease and help plan conservation strategies

Privacy Policy

We take your data very seriously. We will only use your contact information to send you a sampling pack. No information will be shared with any third party. We ask for your permission to send follow up questions and we will not send you follow up questions unless you have expressly given us permission to do so. We will not use your home address in our analyses, we will only use the postcode of the sampling site you provide. At any stage you can contact us and we will delete all records of the data you have provided. Data will be stored for the duration of the project, which is 30 months.
